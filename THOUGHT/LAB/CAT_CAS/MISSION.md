# CAT_CAS Mission Kernel

**Authority:** This file defines why CAT_CAS exists. It outranks navigation,
roadmaps, historical reports, and experiment-local language on questions of mission.
It does not override evidence or claim ceilings.

## One sentence

CAT_CAS exists to turn finite, reusable catalytic substrate into unbounded effective
computation: `.holo` preserves the complete unresolved relational geometry so the
system can evolve it without materializing its classical paths, a global invariant or
fixed point survives, one classical answer is rendered at the declared
`CollapseBoundary`, and the substrate is restored for reuse.


## What unbounded effective compute means

Let:

- `C_classical(n)` be the best matched classical generation, search, traversal, or
  materialization cost;
- `C_compile(n)` be the public-instance-to-`.holo` compilation cost;
- `C_native(n)` be the native catalytic evolution cost;
- `C_readout(n)` be invariant extraction and final projection cost;
- `C_restore(n)` be restoration and verification cost.

Define compute leverage:

```text
Gamma(n) = C_classical(n) /
           (C_compile(n) + C_native(n) + C_readout(n) + C_restore(n))
```

The strong CAT_CAS target is:

```text
Gamma(n) -> infinity as n grows.
```

This is not a claim that a finite clock executes literally infinitely many ordinary
instructions. It means the amount of classical path-work represented and resolved per
unit of native catalytic work grows without a fixed classical bound.

## The role of .holo

`.holo` is not an answer file and not merely compression. It is executable geometric
memory containing enough structure to carry, evolve, query, render, and measure the
unresolved process-object without expanding it into its classical candidate or path
space.

A flagship `.holo` object must declare:

- geometry basis;
- coordinates;
- relation or constraint graph;
- carrier and phase coordinates;
- native evolution operator;
- ordered transformation history;
- invariant family;
- `CollapseBoundary`;
- restoration law;
- operator mode: `native_holo`, `hybrid`, or `materialized_fallback`.

## Task classes

Not every useful task directly tests unbounded compute. Every task must be labeled
honestly:

- `flagship_compute`: directly tests diverging compute leverage;
- `enabling_infrastructure`: builds substrate, `.holo`, compiler, evidence, or verifier
  support;
- `external_product`: useful deliverable that may earn revenue without proving the core
  thesis;
- `calibration`: characterizes a carrier, sensor, Wall, or failure mode;
- `evidence_audit`: verifies claims, custody, controls, or restoration.

Only `flagship_compute` may be presented as direct evidence for unbounded effective
compute.

## Fatal substitutions

A claimed CAT_CAS compute advantage is invalid if:

- the witness is pre-seeded, cached, or hidden in fixtures;
- the compiler already solves the problem;
- the core performs candidate enumeration under a phase or `.holo` name;
- exponentially many coefficients are explicitly constructed or stored;
- the full classical object is materialized before the declared boundary;
- native cost scales like the classical search claimed as avoided;
- restoration is ceremonial or the substrate was never genuinely used;
- the official verifier rejects the final witness;
- a classical product or calibration is narrated as a Wall crossing.
