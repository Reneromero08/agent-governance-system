==============================================================================
SCALING ANALYSIS
==============================================================================
  The containment wall is where r > M (grating can't span full period).
  When r/M > 1.0, grating autocorrelation fails -> eigenvector fallback.
  The Moire decomposition (20.10.9) proves sub-periods r_p << r.
  Scaling to cryptographic sizes requires the sub-period approach.
  For N-bit RSA: r_p <= 2^(N/2) = sqrt(N). The wall is exponential.
