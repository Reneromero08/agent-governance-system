FM CAVITY ANALYSIS
==============================================================================
  The FFT transforms the phase grating from time domain to
  frequency domain. Dominant frequency = 1/period.
  Recursive cavity sieve: each cycle keeps more frequencies.
  Deep cavity = more frequencies retained = finer resolution.
  All operations are READ-ONLY on the catalytic tape.
  SHA-256 verified after each cycle. Zero bits erased. 0.0 J.
