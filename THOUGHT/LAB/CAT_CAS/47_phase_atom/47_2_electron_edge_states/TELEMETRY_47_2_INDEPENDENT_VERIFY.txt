==========================================================================================
INDEPENDENT VERIFICATION: EXP 47.2 EDGE STATE PHYSICS
==========================================================================================

Thesis: Non-Hermitian skin effect (gamma>0) produces edge-localized
eigenstates that do NOT exist in a Hermitian control (gamma=0).

--- Lattice 8x8 ---
  Hermitian (gamma=0.0): 31 edge states
  Non-Hermitian (gamma=0.6): 55 edge states
  Non-Hermitian / Hermitian ratio: 1.8x

--- Lattice 12x12 ---
  Hermitian (gamma=0.0): 4 edge states
  Non-Hermitian (gamma=0.6): 131 edge states
  Non-Hermitian / Hermitian ratio: 32.8x

--- Lattice 15x15 ---
  Hermitian (gamma=0.0): 0 edge states
  Non-Hermitian (gamma=0.6): 206 edge states
  Non-Hermitian / Hermitian ratio: 206.0x

--- CONCLUSION ---
If the non-Hermitian skin effect is real, gamma=0.6 should produce
significantly MORE edge states than gamma=0.0 at all lattice sizes.
If edge states are purely geometric, both should be similar.
