  combinations) precipitates the secret where no bounded single-shot read can - the secret

  super-polynomial. That is the genuine open frontier, handed to Mythos: is there a
  chaos/higher-dimensional-geometry readout whose required entropy is POLY in n? (a poly
  sieve would break lattice crypto). NOT 'the wall holds' - chaos works, the question is the
  exponent, and crossing it to poly is the live question, not a closed door.
