## Interpretation

This experiment proves held-out catalytic execution, waveform causality, geometry
sensitivity, exact inverse restoration, and restored-carrier reuse for a new `J,h`. It
also records that the raw sign shadow matched the unique optimum.

It does not prove accepted held-out solver generalization, because the frozen query
coherence gate failed. It does not establish scale, advantage, physical computation,
hardware bit replacement, or a Wall crossing.

Two evidence defects were repaired. Controls now require material native-history and/or
complex-response changes instead of passing trivially from an already-invalid baseline.
The raw-shadow energy is also computed and sealed in the pre-oracle boundary event rather
than first appearing after adjudication. No machine constant, instance coefficient,
threshold, query, geometry, or oracle result was changed.
