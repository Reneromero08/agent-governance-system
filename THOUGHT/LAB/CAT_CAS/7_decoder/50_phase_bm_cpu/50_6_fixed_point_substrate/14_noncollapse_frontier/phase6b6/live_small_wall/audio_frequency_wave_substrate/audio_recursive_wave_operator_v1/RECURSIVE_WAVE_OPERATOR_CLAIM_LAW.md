# Recursive Wave Operator Claim Law

**Status:** `FROZEN_R1_CLAIM_CEILING`

## Allowed Result

After exact R0 integration, repaired source-custody verification, committed-byte
trajectory reproduction, strict step and receipt closure, AST non-collapse proof, four
independent reviews, and changed-path qualification, this package may emit only the R1
result defined by the package contract.

Its meaning is bounded to an ordinary-software reference in which:

```text
the complete prior recursive phase tree and complete drive tree remain executable
subtrees under every new temporal phase root;
T0-T3 are canonical, deterministic, order-sensitive, unit-modulus, and committed;
exact state-role receipt traversal recovers the committed T0 bytes;
flat-wave and decoded-spin controls fail native tree and receipt admission;
no collapsed scalar reaches the native recurrence call graph
```

Claim ceiling:

```text
SOFTWARE_COMPLETE_TREE_TEMPORAL_RECURRENCE_REFERENCE_ONLY
```

## Non-Collapse Law

The native path is exactly the AST-reachable call graph rooted at:

```text
temporal_step
trajectory
extract_predecessor
```

It must not reach matched response, spin decoding, energy evaluation, FFT magnitude,
`argmin`, `argmax`, flat/spin baselines, candidate selection, winner selection, or an
expected-answer field. Diagnostics and collapsed controls remain outside that graph.

This proof is authority over exact committed source evaluated in a fresh process. It is
not a claim of tamper resistance after import, hostile-interpreter isolation, or safety
against an actor already authorized to monkeypatch live Python objects. Qualification
binds and checks both the R1 source and the exact imported R0 source before trusting the
fresh-process result.

## Ancestry Is Not Catalysis

Exact reverse receipt traversal proves bounded structural ancestry. It does not prove a
catalytic loop. R1 retains its complete history and latches no surviving result outside
the history being traversed.

## Forbidden Promotion

This package does not establish:

```text
software or physical catalytic-loop closure
Ising coupling, solving, or optimization
physical phase-native or audio computing
physical carrier persistence or restoration
algorithmic, energy, or capacity advantage
bit replacement in hardware
Small Wall or Big Wall crossing
```

## Failure Law

The R1 result is forbidden if any frozen source identity fails, R0 does not reproduce,
any committed tree/WAV/spec/receipt byte fails its manifest binding, exact T0 recovery
fails, order sensitivity misses its prospective bound, a collapsed control enters native
admission, the AST proof finds scalar feedback, any required adversary is admitted, a
material review finding remains, or repository custody fails.
