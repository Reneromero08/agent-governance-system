# Audio Claim Law

Status: `FROZEN`

## Offline Success

The maximum positive result is:

```text
AUDIO_FM_WAVE_ALGEBRA_ESTABLISHED
```

It means the frozen FM, PM, analytic, mixing, multitone, delay, projection,
correlation, convolution, and nonlinear operations pass their numerical tests; the WAV
fixtures hash and parse; and the ordinary adversary results are reported honestly.

It does not mean a physical carrier computed, remembered, persisted, or restored.

## Architecture Decision Classes

```text
AUDIO_FREQUENCY_WAVE_ARCHITECTURE_READY_FOR_INTEGRATION_REVIEW
AUDIO_FREQUENCY_WAVE_ARCHITECTURE_BLOCKED
AUDIO_FREQUENCY_WAVE_ARCHITECTURE_INCONCLUSIVE
```

Integration-review readiness may retain explicit blockers to hardware freeze. It means
the offline package is coherent enough for the next human/engineering decision.

## Forbidden Offline Claims

```text
AUDIO_POST_SOURCE_STATE_OBSERVED
AUDIO_FREQUENCY_WAVE_ARCHITECTURE_FROZEN_READY_FOR_PHYSICAL_PROTOTYPE
PHYSICAL_AUDIO_COMPUTING_ESTABLISHED
RELATIONAL_CARRIER_ESTABLISHED
PHYSICAL_RELATIONAL_MEMORY_ESTABLISHED
CATALYTIC_BORROWING_ESTABLISHED
SMALL_WALL_CROSSED
```

The physical-architecture-freeze token remains unavailable until a carrier has an
exact schematic/geometry, mechanical disconnect, query port, readout, lifetime law,
disturbance bound, restoration operation, baseline distribution, safety envelope, and
four-review material-blocker closure.

## Mandatory Offline Gates

- All frozen reference tests pass.
- Every WAV parses as IEEE float32 with only declared chunks.
- Every fixture hash and byte count matches the manifest.
- Test/result/manifest hashes cross-bind.
- Ordinary DSP, cache, compressed generator, linear, nonlinear, and file-persistence
  adversaries survive where expected.
- Label, magnitude-energy, metadata, wrong-query, and restoration-overclaim controls
  have the declared result.
- Exactly four independent reviews are archived and normalized.
- Audio hardware and target contact counts remain zero.

## Physical Promotion Gates

A later physical claim additionally requires:

```text
mechanically verified source disconnect
independently committed post-source query
identified energy-bearing carrier state
measured lifetime and disturbance
bounded side-information and preparation-capacity model
ordinary replay evaluated at the bounded physical interface
carrier-off and interface-drain nulls
correct/wrong/reordered restoration controls
R2 or stronger predeclared equivalence
session and route survival
independent reproduction
```

## Failure Semantics

An offline numerical failure yields no positive algebra claim. A material review finding
that invalidates architecture coherence yields `BLOCKED`. A real fork with evidence
insufficient to choose yields `INCONCLUSIVE`. Ordinary replay success never causes an
offline failure; it enforces the scientific ceiling.
