# Catalytic Wave Loop Claim Law

**Status:** `FROZEN_R2S_CLAIM_CEILING`

## Allowed result

After exact R0/R1 reproduction, strict contract/latch/closure custody, committed-byte
carrier replay, correct and wrong restoration closure, AST no-feedback proof, four PASS
reviews, zero open material findings, and branch qualification, the package may emit:

```text
AUDIO_SOFTWARE_CATALYTIC_WAVE_LOOP_ESTABLISHED
```

Its meaning is limited to an ordinary-software reference in which:

```text
the committed R0 complex carrier is nontrivially displaced by committed R1 T1-T3;
one public query selected before trajectory execution produces a complex latch;
the latch remains outside native forward, inverse, and ancestry histories;
the correct reverse schedule restores the carrier to the frozen 1e-12 equivalence region;
exact R1 receipt traversal recovers committed T0 canonical bytes;
wrong, reordered, omitted, duplicated, shifted, and no-restore controls fail;
no latch scalar reaches the native carrier call graph
```

Claim ceiling:

```text
SOFTWARE_CATALYTIC_WAVE_LOOP_REFERENCE_ONLY
```

## Restoration language

The before and restored carrier hashes differ. The claim is numerical equivalence
restoration, never byte restoration. `carrier_byte_exact` remains a required honest
diagnostic.

## Forbidden promotion

This result does not establish:

```text
byte-exact carrier restoration
recursive catalytic Ising or optimization
physical carrier persistence or restoration
physical audio or silicon-phononic computing
capacity, speed, energy, or algorithmic advantage
hardware bit replacement
Small Wall or Big Wall crossing
```

## Failure law

The established token is forbidden if a parent or fixture identity fails, the query is
selected after result observation, the carrier is not displaced, the correct inverse
misses tolerance, any required wrong arm restores inside `0.05`, T0 bytes do not recover,
the latch changes or feeds native evolution, the closure retains reversed history, a
material review finding remains, or repository custody fails.
