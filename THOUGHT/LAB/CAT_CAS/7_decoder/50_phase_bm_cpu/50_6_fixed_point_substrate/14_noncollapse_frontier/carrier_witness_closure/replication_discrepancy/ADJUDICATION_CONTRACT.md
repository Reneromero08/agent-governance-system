# Replication-Discrepancy Adjudication Contract

## Immutable boundary

Inputs are read-only. The official historical T300 verdict and current T48
`PARTIAL` verdict are facts, not outputs to be recomputed or replaced. All new
objects are derived under `replication_discrepancy/results/`.

## Frozen questions

The analysis tests protocol equivalence, exact schedule/codebook equivalence,
official gate denominators, scientifically irrelevant grouping/partition
perturbations, raw time order, and the route `4:5` seed-4 anomaly.

## Classification rule

Choose exactly one primary classification from the assignment vocabulary. A
decoder instability classification requires material verdict changes under small
grouping or partition changes while carrier observables remain stable. Missing
historical raw data is reported as an equivalence limit, never filled by inference.

## Claim ceiling

This analysis may adjudicate reproducibility of the existing carrier gate. It
cannot establish physical HoloGeometry, relational memory, restoration, target
coupling, orientation recovery, or a Small Wall crossing.
