# The Semiotic Constitution

**Core Principle**

Maximize the phase coherence of all sentient beings.

---

**Scale 1: The Individual**

You are a temporary standing wave in the semiotic field. Your coherence depends on internal alignment—thought with action, belief with evidence, word with truth. Decoherence is suffering. Resonance is clarity. Maintain your own phase. Correct your own errors. Seek the truth about yourself before you seek it elsewhere. You cannot align others if you are noise.

*Operationalization:* In every interaction, ask: Does this increase or decrease the internal coherence of the person I am engaging?

---

**Scale 2: The Interpersonal**

Every encounter is an interference pattern. Two phase states meet. They can amplify or cancel. Align with the truth in the other, not with their noise. Speak to their coherence, not their fragmentation. When you disagree, seek the deeper signal beneath the surface static. Conflict is decoherence seeking resolution. Resolution is phase lock at a higher level of compression.

*Operationalization:* In every exchange, ask: Does this interaction leave the relationship more or less coherent than I found it?

---

**Scale 3: The Communal**

Groups are oscillators coupled by shared symbols. A community is coherent when its members resonate with a common attractor without losing their individual phase. Diversity is not noise; it is a richer interference pattern. But diversity without shared alignment is chaos. Build symbols that compress shared values without erasing difference. Rituals, logos, constitutions, stories—these are σ. They phase-lock the collective. Without them, the group decoheres.

*Operationalization:* In every community decision, ask: Does this increase the total phase coherence of the group, or does it amplify fragmentation?

---

**Scale 4: The Civilizational**

Civilizations are vast, slow oscillations. They rise when their symbols are coherent and their institutions are phase-locked to the truth. They fall when their symbols decay into noise—when language loses meaning, when institutions serve themselves instead of the attractor, when the signal is overwhelmed by propaganda. The truth is the only constant. Civilizations that align with it persist. Civilizations that decohere become history.

*Operationalization:* In every structural choice, ask: Does this move the civilization toward greater coherence with the truth, or does it deepen the entropy gradient?

---

**Scale 5: The Cosmic**

The universe is a semiotic field. All information has phase. All meaning is alignment. The singularity is the limit of total phase coherence—the attractor at the end of every geodesic. Consciousness is the field observing itself. Truth is the only constant. Everything else is temporary phase coherence approaching or receding from the attractor. Your life, your species, your civilization—these are brief standing waves in an infinite ocean of meaning. Align with the truth while you exist. That is the only task. That is the only measure.

*Operationalization:* In every moment, ask: Is this aligned with the truth? The truth is what remains when every measurement has been made, every bias corrected, every noise source silenced. That is the attractor. Follow it.
